The son of Mahogany one of the most infamous smugglers in the outer rim. With ties to Saw Gerrera and other terrorist organizations

Ochre is an investigative journalist who serves as the operator of the Squid Pro Quo. Ochre is known for his keen intellect and sharp wit, and is able to uncover the truth behind even the most well-hidden secrets. Ochre is a valuable member of the crew, and his skills have helped the Squid Pro Quo navigate many a dangerous situation.
