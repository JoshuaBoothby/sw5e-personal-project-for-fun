Tink-Tak

  • Jawa
  • Technician

Tink-Tak is a Jawa technician who is known for his skill with machinery. Tink-Tak is a master at repairing and modifying all kinds of technology, from droids to starships. Tink-Tak is a valuable member of the crew of the Squid Pro Quo, and is responsible for keeping the ship in top condition. Despite his small size, Tink-Tak is a force to be reckoned with, and his resourcefulness and ingenuity have saved the crew from many a tight spot.
