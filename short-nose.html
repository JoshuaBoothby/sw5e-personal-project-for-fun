Short Nose

Deceased

Short Nose was a skilled sniper, muscle, and scout who was a member of the crew of the Squid Pro Quo. Short Nose was known for his keen eye and steady hand, and was able to take out targets with deadly accuracy. Short Nose was also a formidable fighter, and was able to hold his own in hand-to-hand combat. Short Nose was a valuable member of the crew, and his loss was keenly felt by all who knew him.
