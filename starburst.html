5t-ar3urst is an independent droid who has been reprogrammed to serve as the engineer aboard the Squid Pro Quo. 5t-ar3urst is a skilled mechanic and is able to repair and maintain the ship's engines with ease. 5t-ar3urst is known for being efficient and reliable, and is an invaluable member of the crew. Despite being a droid, 5t-ar3urst has developed a personality of its own, and is known for its dry sense of humor and quick wit.(filler text)
